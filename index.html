Cordillera del Paine, Chile

In 2013 the Torres del Paine National Park was named the fifth most beautiful place in the world by National Geographic. Best time to go. The park is open year-round; however, the best season to visit is from October to April.

Dettifoss, Iceland

Dettifoss is reputed to be the most powerful falls in Europe, having a flow estimated at between 200–500 cubic metres of water per second, depending on the summer ice melt. Best time to go. June to September—roads are not in service from January until the beginning of April.

Machu Picchu, Peru

Often referred to as ‘The Lost City of the Incas’, Machu Picchu is perhaps the most familiar icon of the Inca World. It is situated above the Urubamba Valley in Peru, 80 km northwest of Cuzco and through which the Urubamba River flows..
